Frequently asked questions

Is the Check scan really free?

Yes. You can scan a page against 50+ WCAG 2.1 AA criteria and get a Legal Risk Level report instantly, with no sign-up and no cost.

Can I buy just one solution?

Absolutely. Each solution stands on its own. Many organizations start with one and add others as their accessibility program grows.

Why custom quotes instead of fixed prices?

Accessibility needs vary widely. Pricing is based on your organization size, the number of documents and pages involved, and the tools you choose — so you only pay for what fits.

Will this help with the April 2027 ADA Title II deadline?

Yes. The suite is built around WCAG 2.1 AA, the standard tied to ADA Title II. Start by auditing, then monitor and remediate, and document conformance with a VPAT/ACR.
