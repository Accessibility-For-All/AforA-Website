## Verdict
Starter at $97/mo is the unanimous plan call across all five hats — the deliberately inbound-webhook-only architecture uses nothing a higher tier sells — but nothing should be purchased until custody is answered: nobody on the A4A/Blend side knows whose agency account the A4A location lives under. The GHL build is neither launch-ready nor launch-blocking: the John email is still unsent, "mostly complete" is unverified, and the purchase path has no named owner, no failed-payment handling, no idempotency, and no authenticated sending domain. Ship the site Thursday, but flip `selfServe:true` only when the A4A Stripe account is confirmed live and branded, an intake owner is named for fair weekend, and the privacy/terms pages are true.

## GHL plan recommendation
**Buy: Starter — $97/mo** (uses 1 of 3 locations; unlimited users/contacts/workflows). Realistic all-in **~$110–160/mo**: $97 + LC Email (~$0.68/1k sends) + metered Inbound Webhook premium executions (pennies at A4A volume) + $0 SMS until A2P and consent exist. The whole integration — site forms, scanner, and the planned Cloudflare Worker — POSTs to workflow webhook URLs and never reads the REST API, which is exactly what makes Starter (effectively no meaningful API access) sufficient and keeps zero readable CRM credentials in existence.

- **Runner-up: Unlimited — $297/mo.** The trigger: the day the Worker graduates from webhook POSTs to two-way REST calls (idempotent contact/opportunity upserts, pipeline stage moves, provisioning automation, a customer dashboard) — several High findings below are solved more robustly that way — or a 4th location if consolidated under one Blend agency. It's a plan toggle, not a migration; treat the upgrade as a security/key-issuance event, not just billing.
- **Never: Agency Pro ($497)** — SaaS-resale/rebilling machinery, categorically irrelevant to a one-location business selling its own software.
- **Defer:** annual billing (~$970/yr ≈ 2 months free) until 60–90 days of proven fit AND custody resolved (a Summer-2026 50%-off-3-months promo may apply to a new account); the **AI Employee add-on ($97/mo/location)** — pay-per-use Conversation AI (~$0.02/msg) is cheaper until ~4,850 msgs/mo and no chatbot is scoped or WCAG-vetted.
- **Purchase precondition — custody:** if the A4A location rides under Blend's (or worse, John's) existing agency plan, A4A may need to buy no plan at all — but the contact list is the marketing asset this build exists to create, so ownership/export rights must be written down wherever it lives. Default: a standalone A4A-owned Starter account keeps A4A independent.
- **External Stripe Checkout stands** (client-owned account, PCI off-plate, no GHL lock-in). Its named costs — CRM blindness until the Worker ships, and attribution becoming the site's job — are findings 6–8 below, not reasons to switch to GHL payments.
