Frequently asked questions

Is the Check scan really free?

Yes. You can scan a page against 50+ WCAG 2.1 AA criteria and get a Legal Risk Level report instantly, with no sign-up and no cost.

What happens after I purchase?

You'll get an immediate confirmation, and our team reaches out within one business day to set up your sites, documents, and monitoring. You're never left staring at an empty dashboard.

Can I change or cancel my plan?

Yes — upgrade, downgrade, or cancel any time. Annual plans include two months free compared to monthly billing.

What's the difference between the ACR draft and a signed ACR/VPAT?

The automated draft (Growth and up) is a machine-generated conformance report — great for internal tracking. A signed ACR/VPAT adds certified human review and a signature you can hand to procurement. Scale includes one per year; on other plans it's a per-report add-on.

Will this help with the April 2027 ADA Title II deadline?

Yes. The suite is built around WCAG 2.1 AA, the standard tied to ADA Title II. Start by auditing, then monitor and remediate, and document conformance with a VPAT/ACR.
