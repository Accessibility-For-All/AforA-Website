Reviewer Studio

Certified human review, built in

Turn an automated scan into a certified report. Reviewer Studio leverages trained accessibility experts to solve what AI cannot.

For each criterion a machine can't judge, it explains why a human is needed, gives step-by-step "how to check it" guidance, and pinpoints the exact spots the scan flagged. Reviewers record a verdict with a single keypress, a "Draft with AI" helper turns quick notes into polished report language, and a live completion meter tracks progress through every item that needs review.
